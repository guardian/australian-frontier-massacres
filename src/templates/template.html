The Frontier Wars

The Australian frontier wars is a term applied by some historians to the fatal encounters between First Nation peoples and white settlers during the British colonisation of Australia. The first massacres took place shortly after the landing of the First Fleet in January 1788 and continued into the first half of the 20th century.
